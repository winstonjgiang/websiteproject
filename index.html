A Little Bit About Me

Hi! My name is Sammi! I am based in Orange County.
I've been baking all sorts of pastries since I was 8 years old. I also enjoyed drawing
so I would make my own templates for macarons and sell custom-made macarons to friends or family for special occassions!
I started baking at the peak of the quarantine to financially help my parents but eventually,
I decided to take it even further and turn it into a full business! Slowly, it
gradually evolved into my own little small business within a few years to where we are now!


Please don't be afraid to send me a DM for anything business-related!
